Patient Education

Knee Pain & Osteoarthritis

Knee osteoarthritis is a common cause of pain in older adults. Early treatment with physiotherapy, lifestyle changes, and medications can help avoid surgery. In advanced cases, knee replacement may be required.

Rotator Cuff Injuries

Shoulder pain and weakness may be due to rotator cuff tears. Arthroscopic repair is a minimally invasive procedure that restores function and reduces pain.

Sports Injuries

Common sports injuries include ligament tears (ACL, MCL), meniscus injuries, and ankle sprains. Early diagnosis and proper rehabilitation are key to a full recovery.

Clubfoot in Children

Clubfoot is a birth defect where one or both feet turn inward. With early treatment, including casting and surgery if needed, children can lead normal active lives.

Ankle Sprains

Ankle sprains are one of the most common injuries in sports. Proper rest, ice, compression, and elevation (RICE) are important for recovery. Severe cases may require surgery.
